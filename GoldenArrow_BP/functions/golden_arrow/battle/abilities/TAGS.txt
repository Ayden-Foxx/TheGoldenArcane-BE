g_arrow:

• dashing - melee/special
GA is dashing. Invisible, speed increased, damage nullified.

• charge_shots - ranged/primary
GA replaces Doom Spears with Charged Dooms. These will apply poison, slowness and summon a lightning strike wherever they hit.

• lock_tick - any
GA's Clock is paused; Functions running will be locked at whatever value the clock was, and no new function will trigger, too.

• lock_weapons - any
Prevents GA from switching weapons with DANCE OF DANGER. For use with special abilities.
